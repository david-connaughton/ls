About Me

"I like the element of surprise with film."

The Irish Times 2015

I am a Photographer based in Dublin Ireland. I shoot my work exclusively on Analogue Cameras with bespoke settings and manual focus lenses. My work is faithful to colour film and uses only ambient lighting.
I am a multi-award winning artist, winning The Irish Times Colour Photographer of The Year Award in 2013 & 2015.

My images tend to draw on a distinct narrative style. Aspiring to be candid, honest and spontaneous. Unobtrusively capturing the complexity of human expression and emotion within urban incidentals are a hallmark of my work. My images tell a story and will allow your mind to wander. As an initial run, this website contains 12 curated images. Please contact me for purchase and licensing enquiries. Extensive catalogues of my work are also available on my social channels.
